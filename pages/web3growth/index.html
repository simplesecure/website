There’s No Silver Bullet

While there are plenty of places one might focus, Web3 founders have discovered that there isn’t a magic formula for solving the ecosystem’s growth problem. They shown an understanding of the multi-faceted challenges ahead of them.

Devin Finzer, CEO @ OpenSea

"Expectations had to be adjusted down after CryptoKitties. There was the feeling that everyone was just going to start buying NFTs, but we learned that people don’t just buy because an NFT it's on a blockchain. What drives value are utility and provenance."

Long-Term Focus on Growth

The early days of Web3 continue to be slow moving, but new products and tools come to market seemingly everyday. Founders that leverage those tools can better accelerate growth.

Joe Lallouz, CEO @ Bison Trails

“We have to continue doing the unscalable things and continue to be patient around early days of growth. Growth happens, but timing also matters. The most transformative technology didn’t happen overnight even though it feels like it now.”
Share Blurb

Bison Trails

Bison Trails is the easiest way to run secure infrastructure on multiple blockchains. Our technology platform provides enterprise-grade security, multi-cloud / multi-region distribution, and a 99.9% node uptime guarantee.

A simple interface to access decentralized finance to invest, earn interest and borrow crypto assets.

Evgeny Yurtaev, CEO @ Zerion

“It all comes down to finding a product/market fit. It’s not special to our team. It’s the same for everyone. Finding the user that will be the driver of virality of the product. Retention is important. We relied on Twitter for growth, and it was a good solution to generating growth. The next step is to figure out how to break out of the small community.”

2020 Web3 Growth Predictions

With optimism mixed with caution, founders in Web3 almost universally understand that 2020 is a turning point for the entire ecosystem. They are torn on what will be the sub-category of Web3 to grow the most, but they believe growth will come.

Alan Curtis, CEO @ Radar

“If programmatic trust is a value proposition to your company, you’re going to be migrating to blockchain. That may not happen in 2020, but you’re going to see the seed being planted.”
Share Blurb

Radar

Building products for our next financial system.

Learn More

Terminal

Manage & monitor your dapp. One mission control panel for your blockchain application.

Learn More

Harrison Hines, CEO @ Terminal

“My prediction is that there will be an explosion in the use of decentralized storage. With Filecoin going live and infrastructure being built around IPFS, there will be more opportunity to use decentralized storage. A lot of dapps are centralized in data storage still. There will be growth in the category of dapps, not just ones using smart contracts. Many might just use decentralized infrastructure plus IPFS or 3Box.”
Share Blurb

Samyak Jain, CTO @ InstaDapp

“I’m really bullish on DeFi. If someone were to release YouTube in 2000, it wouldn’t have worked because of scalability problems that would have been present. The same thing applies to most blockchain applications. Right now, DeFi can solve real problems without many of the scalability issues other products would have.”
